Arriving at the docklands the investigators see graffiti on a wall inside the abandoned warehouse: a crude stag symbol with the words _"The Children rise"_ in Welsh. Nearby, a discarded cloak bears an embroidered stag emblem.

As the investigators approach they can hear chanting in ancient Brythonic. A group of the cult led by [[Sister Morwenna Trelawny|Sister Morwenna ]] is creating one of [[the Reborn]].

If overheard, Morwenna prays: _"May the Horned One enter his chosen skin smoothly. May the Stag‑King rise without tearing his vessel asunder."_

The ritual is horrific to see Sanity Loss 1d4/1d10 involving sacrifice blood and salt with the resulting undead a creature of horror.

Found in the cult's hidden grove: a ceremonial calendar with Samhain (Oct 31) circled in blood. Notes describe the "convergence of realms" and the "gateway opening at midnight."

Sister Mowenna has a small bible that has been desecrated with "Lies" and "There is no god but the Stag" scrawled on its pages. The dedication in the front reads "To Sister Morwenna Trelawny without whom I would not be still among the living. May the ghosts of Verdun haunt you no more. G. Chester". Her silver crucifix is also inscribed with a single word "Verdun".
