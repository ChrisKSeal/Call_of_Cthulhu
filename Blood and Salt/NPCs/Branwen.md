## Appearance (When Manifesting)

When Branwen's spirit briefly overcomes Gwen's form, witnesses see:

- Eyes that change colour, becoming grey green and burning with inner fire, reflecting flames that died two millennia ago
- Hair that moves as if blown by unfelt winds
- Skin marked with blue woad tattoos that appear and fade like smoke
- Voice that carries the authority of ancient sacred groves
- Presence that makes the air itself feel heavy with age and power
- Regal stance quite different to that of [[Gwen]]

## Personality

Branwen embodies the paradox of ancient wisdom twisted by centuries of rage. She is simultaneously a learned scholar of cosmic forces and a vengeful spirit consumed by injustice. Her possession of Gwen has given her access to modern knowledge, which she uses to justify her ancient purposes with contemporary logic.

- **Intellectually Brilliant:** Masters both ancient druidic lore and modern academic thought
- **Morally Complex:** Genuinely believes her actions serve cosmic justice, not mere revenge
- **Emotionally Volatile:** Switches between maternal wisdom and burning hatred
- **Strategically Patient:** Has waited twenty centuries for the perfect moment and vessel

## Backstory

**Life (c. 20-60 CE):**  
Born into the sacred bloodline of Anglesey's ruling druids, Branwen was trained from childhood in the deepest mysteries. She witnessed the increasing Roman pressure on Celtic lands and foresaw the coming catastrophe. As Arch-Druidess, she led the resistance against Romanization and prepared the final defenses of Ynys Môn.

**Death (60 CE):**  
When Suetonius Paulinus attacked Anglesey, Branwen led the final ritual defense. As Roman soldiers burned the sacred groves, she invoked a great curse with her dying breath, binding her spirit to the cosmic wound created by the cultural genocide. Her death was not just personal—it was the symbolic death of an entire way of life.

**The Long Wait (60-1925 CE):**  
For nearly two millennia, Branwen's spirit has watched the bloodlines of both conqueror and conquered mingle and evolve. She has observed how Roman families integrated into Celtic society, how their descendants forgot their origins, and how the cosmic injustice remained unresolved. She waited for the perfect vessel—someone carrying both bloodlines who could bring about her final vengeance.

## Motivations

**Primary Goal:** Summon Cernunnos to restore the old cosmic order through willing sacrifice  
**Secondary Goal:** Exact vengeance for the cultural genocide of her people  
**Deeper Purpose:** Heal the metaphysical wound created when Roman steel met Celtic sacred space

**The Sacred Paradox:**  
Branwen has resolved @Gwen Cadwallon's mixed heritage as the perfect solution to cosmic justice. Only someone carrying both Roman guilt and Celtic pain can truly bring about her vengeance.

## Powers and Abilities
**Spiritual Possession:** Complete control over Gwen's body and access to her memories and knowledge  
**Ancient Knowledge:** Remembers the true names and natures of pre-Roman British gods  
**Ritual Magic:** Can perform Celtic ceremonies with authentic ancient power  
**Cult Leadership:** Inspires fanatical devotion in those seeking connection to ancient Britain  
**Cauldron Mastery:** Understands the true power of the Cauldron of Rebirth
