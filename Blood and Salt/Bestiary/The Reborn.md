The Reborn appear as pale, gaunt versions of their former selves, with skin that has a distinctive white, crystalline sheen from the salt preservation process. Their throat wounds remain visible as dark, bloodless gashes that never heal. Salt crystals can be seen embedded in their flesh, particularly around joints and the wound site. Their eyes retain some spark of their former personality but show an otherworldly devotion to their cause.
