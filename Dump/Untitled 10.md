## **Handout Usage Guidelines**

### **Timing:**

- **#1-4:** Given during initial investigation phase
- **#5-8:** Revealed as investigators make discoveries
- **#9-11:** Provided during conspiracy revelation phase
- **#12-13:** Final act, building tension
- **#14-15:** Reference materials as needed

### **Presentation Tips:**

- Age paper with tea or coffee staining for authentic feel
- Use different fonts/handwriting for different sources
- Include Celtic symbols and decorative elements
- Burn edges of "ancient" documents carefully
- Use red ink for blood-written messages

### **Player Engagement:**

- Let players examine handouts closely
- Allow time for discussion and theorizing
- Don't explain everything - let them piece clues together
- Use handouts to pace revelation of information
- Make physical props feel significant and mysterious

These handouts provide crucial information while maintaining the mystery and allowing players to feel like genuine investigators uncovering an ancient conspiracy. The mix of official documents, personal letters, and ancient texts creates layers of authenticity that enhance the horror atmosphere.
