# **"The Horn of the Wild Hunt" - THE TRANSFORMATION**

_When Civilized Men Become Beasts_

---

## **BROTHER THORNFIELD'S AVATAR TRANSFORMATION**

### **Stage 1: The Calling (Ritual Begins)**

**Duration:** First 5 minutes of ritual  
**Physical Changes:**

- **Eyes:** Pupils dilate completely, irises turn golden-amber
- **Posture:** Stands unnaturally straight, head tilted as if listening
- **Voice:** Becomes deeper, develops slight echo
- **Breathing:** Slows dramatically, visible vapor in cold air
- **Skin:** Takes on pale, almost translucent quality

**Behavioral Changes:**

- Stops blinking entirely, stares fixedly at the burning Wicker Stag
- Speaks only in ancient Celtic, voice carrying further than should be possible
- Movements become predatory, weight shifted forward on balls of feet
- **Sanity Loss:** 1d4/1d6 (watching respected leader become inhuman)

### **Stage 2: The Growing (Ritual Midpoint)**

**Duration:** Minutes 5-15 of ritual  
**Physical Changes:**

- **Height:** Grows 6 inches taller, clothes strain and tear
- **Musculature:** Visible muscle mass increase, veins prominent
- **Fingers:** Elongate by 2 inches, nails become thick and claw-like
- **Teeth:** Canines extend into fangs, jaw structure subtly changes
- **Hair:** Grows rapidly, becomes coarse and wild
- **Skin Texture:** Becomes rougher, almost bark-like in patches

**Supernatural Manifestations:**

- **Temperature:** Body heat increases dramatically, steam rises from skin
- **Scent:** Strong musk, like wet earth and copper
- **Aura:** Animals within 100 yards flee in terror
- **Voice:** Now carries supernatural authority, cultists obey without question

**Combat Changes:**

- **STR:** Increases by +20 points
- **CON:** Increases by +15 points
- **MOV:** Increases to 12
- **Natural Weapons:** Claws (1d6+damage bonus), Bite (1d4+damage bonus)
- **Sanity Loss:** 1d6/1d8 (human form becoming monstrous)

### **Stage 3: The Antlering (Final Phase)**

**Duration:** Minutes 15-20 of ritual  
**Physical Changes:**

- **Skull:** Forehead bulges outward, temples widen
- **Antlers:** Begin as small bumps, grow rapidly to full rack (4 feet across)
- **Face:** Features become more angular, nose flattens slightly
- **Legs:** Develop additional muscle mass, stance becomes digitigrade
- **Spine:** Elongates, creating more pronounced forward lean
- **Overall Size:** Now 7+ feet tall, massively built

**The Antlers Specifically:**

- **Growth Rate:** 1 foot per minute once they begin
- **Composition:** Living bone, warm to touch, sensitive to air currents
- **Branching:** Seven points on each side (traditional "royal" stag pattern)
- **Supernatural Properties:** Glow faintly in darkness, resonate with Horn of Cernunnos

**Final Form Abilities:**

- **Gore Attack:** 2d6+damage bonus, can impale
- **Supernatural Senses:** See in complete darkness, track by scent across miles
- **Command Presence:** All wild animals within 1 mile come under his control
- **Reality Warping:** Minor alterations to local environment (plants grow, seasons change)

**Sanity Loss:** 1d10/1d15 (complete transformation into inhuman avatar)

## **CULTIST BEAST TRANSFORMATIONS**

### **Voluntary Transformation (Willing Cultists)**

**The Process:** Occurs during minor rituals throughout the campaign

**Stage 1: Awakening (0-30 seconds)**

- Eyes reflect light like an animal's
- Fingernails thicken and darken
- Posture becomes hunched, predatory
- Speech reduces to grunts and growls

**Stage 2: The Change (30-60 seconds)**

- Facial hair grows rapidly into mane-like covering
- Muscle mass increases visibly
- Clothing tears as body expands
- Teeth sharpen to carnivorous points

**Stage 3: Beast Form (Complete)**

- **Appearance:** Hybrid human-beast, retains human intelligence
- **Size:** 6-7 feet tall, heavily muscled
- **Features:** Wolf-like head, human hands with claws, digitigrade legs
- **Duration:** 1 hour per point of POW sacrificed in ritual

**Combat Statistics:**

- **STR:** +10 to human value
- **CON:** +8 to human value
- **DEX:** +5 to human value
- **Natural Weapons:** Bite (1d6), Claws (1d4+1 each)
- **Armor:** 2 points (thick hide)
- **Special:** Enhanced senses, pack tactics

### **Involuntary Transformation (Cursed Victims)**

**The Curse:** Affects anyone who handles the Horn without proper protection

**Day 1-3: Subtle Changes**

- Increased appetite, especially for rare meat
- Enhanced hearing and smell
- Restless sleep, dreams of hunting
- **Mechanical Effect:** +10% Listen and Track

**Day 4-7: Physical Manifestation**

- Body hair growth accelerates
- Canine teeth become more prominent
- Posture changes, prefers crouching
- **Mechanical Effect:** -10% social skills, +20% survival skills

**Day 8-14: The Hunger**

- Craves raw meat, cooked food tastes wrong
- Aggressive behavior toward "prey" animals
- Difficulty with complex technology
- **Mechanical Effect:** Must make POW rolls to resist hunting instincts

**Day 15+: Permanent Change**

- Transformation becomes irreversible without magical intervention
- Retains human memories but animal priorities dominate
- Joins nearest pack (cult cell) for protection and companionship
- **Character becomes NPC** under Keeper control

## **CERNUNNOS MANIFESTATION**

### **The God's Physical Form**

**Initial Appearance (Partial Manifestation):**

- **Height:** 12 feet tall
- **Build:** Powerfully muscled but lean, like a marathon runner
- **Skin:** Deep bronze color with intricate Celtic tattoos that move and shift
- **Face:** Noble but alien, features too sharp and symmetrical for humanity
- **Eyes:** Solid gold, no visible pupils, seem to contain swirling galaxies
- **Antlers:** Massive rack spanning 8 feet, each point carved with spiraling symbols

**Full Manifestation (If Ritual Completes):**

- **Height:** Grows to 15+ feet
- **Presence:** Reality bends around him, perspective becomes non-Euclidean
- **Antlers:** Now 12 feet across, glow with inner fire
- **Aura:** Visible distortion in air, like heat shimmer
- **Voice:** Speaks without moving lips, words appear directly in listeners' minds
- **Movement:** Doesn't walk so much as flow, feet don't always touch ground

### **Environmental Effects of Manifestation**

**100-Yard Radius:**

- All electronic devices fail permanently
- Compasses spin wildly
- Temperature drops 20 degrees
- Plants grow at accelerated rate
- Wild animals gather in reverent silence

**500-Yard Radius:**

- Weather patterns shift dramatically
- Seasonal changes occur instantly (autumn leaves in spring, etc.)
- Stone structures develop cracks along ancient ley lines
- Domestic animals flee or hide
- Sensitive humans feel overwhelming urge to kneel

**1-Mile Radius:**

- All technology functions erratically
- Radio and cell signals completely blocked
- Astronomical phenomena visible in daylight (aurora, etc.)
- Migratory patterns of birds and animals disrupted
- Time flows differently in random pockets

### **The God's Supernatural Abilities**

**Combat Powers:**

- **Antler Gore:** 3d6+10 damage, 10-yard reach
- **Stomp:** 2d6+10 damage, affects 3-yard radius
- **Spectral Pack:** Summons 2d6 ghostly hunting hounds each round
- **Reality Warp:** Can alter local physics (gravity, friction, etc.)

**Divine Abilities:**

- **Master of the Hunt:** Controls all animals within 10 miles
- **Seasonal Command:** Can instantly change local weather/season
- **Life and Death:** Can resurrect recently dead as spectral hunters
- **Dimensional Travel:** Can step between reality layers at will

**Weaknesses:**

- **Silver Weapons:** Take double damage
- **Running Water:** Cannot cross without invitation
- **Iron Circles:** Imprisoned if completely surrounded by cold iron
- **True Names:** Loses power if someone speaks his complete Celtic name correctly

## **PSYCHOLOGICAL TRANSFORMATION EFFECTS**

### **On Witnesses**

**Watching Thornfield Transform:**

- **Academic Investigators:** "All my education means nothing" - 1d8/1d10 Sanity loss
- **Religious Investigators:** "God has abandoned us" - 1d6/1d12 Sanity loss
- **Military Investigators:** "No weapon can stop this" - 1d4/1d8 Sanity loss
- **Medical Investigators:** "This violates every law of biology" - 1d10/1d15 Sanity loss

**Watching Cultist Transformations:**

- **First Time:** 1d6/1d8 Sanity loss
- **Subsequent Times:** 1d2/1d4 Sanity loss (familiarity breeds contempt)
- **If Investigator Knows the Person:** +2 to all Sanity loss rolls

### **On the Transformed**

**Thornfield's Mental State:**

- Retains human memories but reinterprets them through divine perspective
- Views his previous life as "childhood" he has outgrown
- Feels genuine pity for humans still "trapped" in civilization
- Experiences time differently - moments feel like hours, hours like minutes

**Cultist Mental Changes:**

- Human morality replaced by pack loyalty
- Technology becomes incomprehensible and frightening
- Urban environments cause physical discomfort
- Strong urge to recruit family and friends into the "true life"

## **TRANSFORMATION HORROR DETAILS**

### **The Sounds**

- **Bone Growth:** Audible creaking and snapping as skeleton expands
- **Muscle Development:** Wet tearing sounds as tissue stretches
- **Antler Emergence:** Grinding like stone on stone as bone breaks through skin
- **Voice Changes:** Gradual deepening with inhuman harmonics
- **Clothing:** Ripping fabric, popping buttons, snapping leather

### **The Smells**

- **Musk:** Overwhelming animal scent, like a zoo in summer
- **Copper:** Metallic smell of blood from rapid tissue growth
- **Ozone:** Electric smell as reality warps around transformation
- **Earth:** Rich loam odor, as if freshly turned soil
- **Decay:** Hint of rot as human limitations are shed

### **The Visual Horror**

- **Skin Stretching:** Visible distortion as body grows beneath
- **Eyes Changing:** Pupils dilating until whites disappear completely
- **Hair Growth:** Accelerated to time-lapse speed
- **Antler Sprouting:** Skin bulges, splits, bone emerges bloody
- **Posture Shift:** Spine elongating, stance becoming quadrupedal

### **The Wrongness**

- **Proportions:** Human features in wrong relationships to each other
- **Movement:** Too fluid, joints bending in impossible directions
- **Timing:** Some changes happen instantly, others take agonizing minutes
- **Asymmetry:** Transformation doesn't happen evenly across the body
- **Recognition:** Familiar face becoming alien while retaining human elements
